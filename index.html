🎯 Top 5 Content Buckets — Cross-Channel
Themes that consistently outperform across multiple channels and formats · Jul 2025–May 2026
🎬
Film & TV Heritage
Wilmington's on-screen identity creates a perpetual discovery engine. Audiences actively seek out filming locations — content that connects the destination to beloved shows and films converts curiosity into intent at rates no other theme matches.
Evidence across channels: Dawson's Creek IG Reel 19.25% ER · SITP FB post 500K+ impressions · SITP blog post #1 at 7,017 views · filming locations blog 27s avg engagement time
🍴
Foodie & Local Dining
Food content triggers the strongest emotional response and the highest purchase intent of any travel category. When tied to specific local personalities and named chefs, it builds trust and creates a "must try" motivation that general destination content can't replicate.
Evidence across channels: Foodie email automation avg 53% OR / 34.4% CR · Britt's Donuts FB reel 1M+ impressions · Britt's TikTok 37K views · Chef series top 3 campaign click rates
🌊
Wildlife & Nature Moments
Unexpected wildlife sightings and natural phenomena generate a "you had to be there" emotional reaction that drives sharing and saves — the two behaviors most correlated with account growth on Instagram and TikTok. These posts reach non-followers at a disproportionate rate.
Evidence across channels: Whale TikTok Reel 32K views + 3.8K likes (11.9% like rate) · Northern Lights IG posts 15–17% ER across WB, CB, and KB simultaneously · nature posts consistently in top 3 ER per destination
🎉
Local Events & Celebrations
Events create urgency — the content has a natural deadline, which triggers immediate action (saving, sharing, planning). When events feature recognizable local figures or traditions, they combine community pride with destination discovery, reaching both locals and prospective visitors.
Evidence across channels: Queen Azalea TikTok #1 video at 41K views · Azalea Festival LinkedIn 835 imp / 104 clicks (top click rate) · Riverfest FB 100K+ impressions · Britt's reopening 1M+ FB + top TikTok
🏛️
History & Heritage
Heritage content attracts a highly educated, high-intent traveler who researches deeply before booking. On email, this audience self-selects into niche automations and clicks at extraordinary rates. On social, heritage content positions the destination as a place with depth — not just beaches — which expands the addressable travel audience significantly.
Evidence across channels: America 250 email automation avg 60% OR / 59% CR (highest of any content type) · Cape Fear Museum 90s FB Reel 100K+ · "places that make Kure Beach" Reel 285K impressions
